Immediate :
[15:14] [13:11] [10] [9:8] [7:0]
  00      DDD    M   XX   IMM8

DDD  – destination register
M    – mode
XX   – unused
IMM8 – 8-bit value

If M = 0:
Operation: R[DDD][7:0] <= IMM8

If M = 1:
Operation: R[DDD][15:8] <= IMM8
